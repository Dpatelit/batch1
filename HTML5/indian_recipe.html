Paneer Butter Masala Recipe: A Creamy and Rich Delight

This flavorful Paneer Butter Masala recipe will guide you to create a rich and creamy curry with soft paneer cubes cooked in a velvety tomato-based gravy. It's a perfect dish to serve with naan, roti, or rice, and is a favorite among Indian food lovers.

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1-2 green chilies (slit)
  • 2 medium tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional, for sweetness)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Base Gravy:
    Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, and sauté for 2 minutes.
  2. Cook the Tomatoes:
    Add pureed tomatoes to the pan, along with turmeric, red chili powder, and cumin powder. Cook the mixture on medium heat until the oil begins to separate from the masala (spice mixture).
  3. Make the Gravy Creamy:
    Add cream, milk, and sugar (if using) to the pan. Mix well and let it simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add Paneer:
    Gently add the paneer cubes to the gravy. Mix carefully to coat them with the creamy sauce. Cook for another 5 minutes, allowing the paneer to absorb the flavors.
  5. Finish with Kasuri Methi and Garam Masala:
    Sprinkle kasuri methi and garam masala over the curry, and give it a final stir.
  6. Serving:
    Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.

Enjoy Your Homemade Paneer Butter Masala!
This rich and creamy Paneer Butter Masala is perfect for any special meal or to impress guests with its irresistible flavors. Adjust the spice levels and creaminess to your taste and serve it with your favorite Indian bread or rice. Enjoy cooking!

Vegetable Manchurian Recipe: A Spicy, Savory Delight

This recipe will help you make the perfect Vegetable Manchurian, a popular Indo-Chinese dish. These crispy, flavorful vegetable balls are cooked in a rich, spicy sauce that’s perfect as a starter or main dish. Enjoy it with steamed rice or noodles for a complete meal!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, cabbage, beans, finely chopped)
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 tbsp oil (for the sauce)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 small bell pepper (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tsp cornflour mixed with 1 tbsp water (for thickening)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Manchurian Balls:
    In a large bowl, combine the mixed vegetables, all-purpose flour, cornflour, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, soy sauce, vinegar, pepper, and salt. Mix well and shape the mixture into small balls. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the balls until they turn golden brown and crispy. Set aside on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    In another pan, heat 1 tbsp oil. Add the chopped onions and bell peppers, sauté until soft. Add the soy sauce, tomato ketchup, chili sauce, and sugar. Stir well and cook for 2-3 minutes. Then, add the cornflour slurry and cook for another minute to thicken the sauce.
  3. Combine the Balls with the Sauce:
    Add the fried Manchurian balls to the sauce and toss gently to coat the balls in the sauce. Let them cook in the sauce for 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend.
  4. Serving:
    Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot with fried rice or noodles.

Enjoy Your Homemade Vegetable Manchurian!
This delicious and crispy Vegetable Manchurian, cooked in a tangy, spicy sauce, will surely become a favorite at your next meal. Serve it hot with rice or noodles for a delightful experience. Adjust the spice levels according to your preference. Happy cooking!
